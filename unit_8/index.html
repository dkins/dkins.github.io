About

This is our experiment to collect and display our thoughts on race, gender, and living as biracial individuals in the Pacific Northwest. Now a little about us...

ABASI

Abasi is a genderqueer 23-year-old. They enjoy spending their free time volunteering with LGBTQ+ teens, hiking, and exploring Portland's ever-growing music scene. Abasi is passionate about the intersectionalities of race, gender, and sexual orientation.

DIDI

Didi is a 20 something who enjoys the outdoors, riding her motorcycle, and of course her cat. One of her driving forces is to have a positive impact on people's lives. Didi's career led her to the staffing industry where she discovered the movement for diversity in the workplace. Ever since Didi has been a passionate advocate for inclusion, equity, and equality in the workplace.
